All About Turkey!

Turkey: The Republic of Turkey is a country straddling Western Asia and Southeast Europe.
Istanbul, the largest city, is the financial centre, and Ankara is the capital.
Turks form the vast majority of the nation's population, and Kurds are the largest minority.
Its capital is Ankara while its largest city is Istanbul.
